Goal:
  Be the first player to discard all cards in your hand.
On your turn:
  On your turn you can play a card that matches either:
    The suit of the top card of the discard
    The rank of the top card of the discard
Special Cards:
  When you play an 8:
    Pick a suit, the next card played must either:
      Match that suit or
      Be an Eight
To play a card:
  When it is your turn all valid cards will be listed
  To play a card, simply type the number listed next to it.
  As an example, if this was the list of valid cards:
    1. Ace of Spades
    2. Two of Hearts
    3. King of Diamonds
  If you wanted to play the king of diamonds you would type 3
